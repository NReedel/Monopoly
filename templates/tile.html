Tile Structures

Each tile has three main sections: top, middle, and bottom.

The styles of each section is determined by the class of the tile container.

The classes are descriptive of the tile's location in the hierarchy.





Property Tile

div class="tile property prop-purple"

Properties have special classes that determine their tile-top color.

These colors are located in ./css/theme.css instead of ./css/module.css because they might be altered with different board themes later.

Corner Tile - Go

div class="tile special corner go"

The corner class has a 1 / 1 aspect ratio, bigger font sizes, and a smaller tile-top and tile-bottom.

Depending on how we implement the board, I'm thinking the corners might lose their square aspect ratio at some point... at least for mobile.


The go class is unique but actually has the default tile-top font size.

It has unique tile-top and tile-bottom spacing due to its longer top text and unique arrow image at the bottom.
